Privacy Policy for ShopSmart

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

ShopSmart ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address and a securely encrypted password. We do not have access to your password — it is managed securely by Firebase Authentication, a service provided by Google.

App Data

We collect and store the data you enter into the app, including:

  • Store names and details
  • Your master item list
  • Shopping lists and trip history
  • Any notes or product images you add to items

This data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore and is associated with your account.

Usage Data

ShopSmart remembers your shopping preferences and behaviours within the app, such as frequently purchased items and preferred item ordering. This information is stored in your account and is used solely to personalise your experience within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ShopSmart service
  • Personalise your shopping experience
  • Allow sharing of shopping lists between users of the same account
  • Improve the app's functionality over time

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

Your data is only accessible to users who sign in with your account credentials. We do not share your data with any third parties except as necessary to provide the service, specifically Google Firebase which hosts our database and authentication service. Google's privacy policy can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is transmitted over secure encrypted connections and stored securely in Google Firebase. Passwords are never stored in plain text and are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

ShopSmart is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights please contact us at the email address below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
